This is a review and exercise lessons for undergraduate students majoring in physics who has already attained the basic knowledge of Optics. The lessons contains: 
  * Diffraction of Light
  * Polarization and Crystal Optics
  * Dispersion, Scattering, and Absorption
  * Fundamentals of Quantum Optics
