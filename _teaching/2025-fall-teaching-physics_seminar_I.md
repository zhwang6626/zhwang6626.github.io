This course is one of the foundational courses for cultivating top-tier students, targeting first-year students during their transitional phase in university learning. Through flipped classrooms, lectures, and teacher-student seminars, it focuses on developing essential academic competencies in top-tier students, including teamwork, academic expression, knowledge and skills, as well as rigorous and confident scientific literacy. The goal is to equip students with fundamental scientific thinking, basic abilities, core knowledge, and standardized academic expression, thereby laying a solid foundation for their formal engagement in research activities in the second year.

The main content of the course involves identifying physical problems based on specific themes, presenting research findings through independent study, and learning the basic methods and skills of scientific research and academic expression through seminars and instructor-led training in academic presentation.
