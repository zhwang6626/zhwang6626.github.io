COMSOL Multiphysics is a general-purpose simulation software platform that integrates multiple physics fields, including electromagnetics, fluid flow, heat transfer, structural mechanics, acoustics, and chemical engineering. With multiphysics simulation tools, researchers and engineers can more accurately understand and analyze the complex mechanisms underlying various phenomena. This lecture will systematically introduce the basic methods of conducting multiphysics simulations using COMSOL, supported by practical case studies, aiming to help beginners quickly master the software and easily step into the world of multiphysics simulation.
