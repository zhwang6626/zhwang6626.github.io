I was born and raised in Beijing, China. I am currently an undergraduate student majoring in physics at Nankai University. Under the supervision of Prof. [Wang YANG](https://physics.nankai.edu.cn/yw/main.htm), I am conducting research in **theoretical condensed matter physics** and **quantum many-body physics**, focusing on quasi-1D/2D quantum spin chain models and novel quantum magnetism.

Beyond theoretical physics, I am also enthusiastic about **physics education research**. I have served as a teaching assistant of two courses for top-notch students, where I helped reform the curriculum. I am also involved in the **Physics Tournament Visual Teaching and Researching Section (PT VTRS)** of the Ministry of Education in China, where I promote the [PT-RQC teaching and learning model](/_pages/cv.md#Service and leadership).
